TREKSTERS
Adventure Expirences in and around Vizag

Experience the beauty of Eastern ghats in this One Day Nature Expedition by the Treksters.
With us, you get to explore the offbeat and unexplored parts of the Eastern Ghats.
With the monotony and the rat race that we city dwellers are constantly stuck in, an outing like this is the perfect mini vacation and the much required getaway to replenish mental energy—all while making memories with your friends and loved ones on this day off.

01

KONDAKARLAVA
Boating

The day starts early in the morning, reach the assembly point and hop into the bus to start your first leg of journey to the visit a beautiful lake which not only has rich hertiage and history, but is also breathtakingly beautiful and serene on any given time of the day— the Kondakarla Ava located at a distance of 40 odd kilometres from Vizag.

02

KONDAKARLAVA
Birds Sanctuary

Enjoy the sunrise while boating in the lake surrounded by beautiful flowers and exotic birds which have migrated all the way from Siberia, circling around you every now and then. Once you’re done with boating and witnessing one the most beautiful sunrises ever, we will move on towards our journey to an unexplored and an offbeat destination.

03

Yerravaram
Trek to Waterfalls

Once you’re done witnessing ‘the sunrise’ of your life, we will then move on to the magnum opus of the day—Yerravaram. This place, hidden by the mountains of the Eastern Ghats, is the definition of an offbeat yet an intensely beautiful waterfall deep inside the heart of the nature. Trek with us to reach an amazing view point and gaze at the waterfall and the surrounding hills.

04

Lammasingi
Snow Lake

While you’re at the waterfall viewpoint, we’re going to be serving you a lip-smacking desi style lunch. You shall be enjoying a sumptuous meal while chatting with your friends and beholding a stunning scenery. It cant get any better! Can it?

You will be then taken to a beautiful lake in a small village further up in the ghats that comes right after the famous hill station called ‘Lammasingi’. Witness the sunset at the lake and make your way back to Vizag with loads of memories and a refreshing experience in the nature away from the buzz of the daily lives.

05

Treksters​™ ​One​ ​Day​ ​Nature​ ​Expedition
Hike, Trek, Explore and make Memories with us, we’re your perfect getaway
